## KEY TAKEAWAYS

1. **Best overall program for recurring income:** Carrot -- lifetime cookie, 25-30% recurring, proven platform
2. **Highest single commission rate:** BirdDogBot at 50% recurring
3. **Best AI-specific program:** Homesage.ai at up to 40% with 60-day cookie
4. **Easiest to join:** Homesage.ai (no approval process), Virtual Staging AI (1-minute signup)
5. **Programs without public affiliate options:** Follow Up Boss, Ylopo, Rechat -- consider direct outreach for custom partnerships
6. **Non-cash programs:** Styldod (discount-only), Matterport (service partner, not affiliate)
7. **Most common payment method:** PayPal dominates; only LeadsBridge pays via bank transfer
